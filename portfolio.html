NBCC Wellness Web Application


The NBCC Wellness web application was a collaborative project completed as part of my final term at New Brunswick Community College. The goal of this project was to provide an outlet for staff at NBCC to access articles, events, and support services for their physical and mental wellbeing.

Admins of the site can create new content and track the progress of other users. The dashboard serves to show users where most of their time on the site is spent and how much content they have gone through. The Resolution page is where resolutions or tasks added by the admins can be checked off by users as they complete them.

  • Client: New Brunwick Community College
  • Languages: PHP, JavaScript, HTML5, CSS3, MySQL
