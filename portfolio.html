NBCC Wellness Web Application


The NBCC Wellness web application was a collaborative project completed as part of my final term at New Brunswick Community. This project was handed over to the college at the end of the term and is used by staff to keep up to date on health and wellness information and well as college events -- including their famous Rock Your Resolution month-long celebration for health and mental wellbeing.

  • Client: New Brunwick Community College
  • Sprints: Six two-week sprints
  • Languages: PHP, JavaScript, HTML, CSS, SQL
